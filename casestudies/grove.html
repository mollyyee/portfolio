Growth hacking with transparency

Good design should be clear and transparent.

Clarity and transparency can help businesses achieve their business goals. I redesigned a referral flow that drastically improved our conversion rates.

Impact

The redesign quadrupled page conversions and increase site traffic through referrals by 50%.

Product

Grove is a green eCommerce supbscription service that sells natural home products in recurring shipments.

The original design required too much guesswork.



Unlike other typical referral flows, we required users to share the referral link twice, through two modes of social media before receiving their reward. Looking above, it's hard to tell that that's the expectation.



Ideating to visually setting expectations.

One research participant exclaimed, "Hey, that's sneaky!" in one of three rounds of usability tests with 14 people. People weren't used to having to do two things when referring their friends. We had to be extra clear and lead users by presenting the next step's clear call to action.


Then converge.

These are the final designs we landed on based on 3 rounds of usability tests on 14 people. You could check it out live at grove.co/referral/share.
