Automating operations to enable an assisted-driving datacenter


After recruiting a forum of 165 end users and learning more about their frustrations with optimization for 8 weeks, I found that users were in need of reliable, automated processes in their datacenter.

The north star is to take our Operations tool into the future by enabling a self-driving, intelligent datacenter, that operates and remediates itself.

My project is part of our strategic path there: Let’s optimize infrastructure, so that preventable performance issues are mitigated before they occur. And also automate it, so that an IT team never has to fix the same issue twice.
