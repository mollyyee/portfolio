A Curvy Console
Plywood, bandsaw, jigsaw and a lot of wood glue

Stacked cross sections to create curved legs

I considered either approaching the console by wood bending or by stacking cross sections. Because I wanted a more dramatic curve at a thicker width, I went with cutting cross sections in the shapes I wanted and stacking them together.

With some metal plates and bolts along with glue and filler, the console was standing!

Then it shaped up with lots of sanding, filling, and painting the hand-cut pieces.
